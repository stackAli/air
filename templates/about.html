About Our Aviation College

Soaring Towards Excellence

Founded with a commitment to shape the future of aviation, our college stands as a premier institute offering internationally accredited programs in pilot training.

Our purpose-built campus includes flight simulators, hangars, and interactive classrooms—ensuring our students receive the most immersive and hands-on training experience possible.

If you aim to become a pilot we are here to launch your aviation journey with integrity and excellence.

Why Train at Moorabbin Airport?

Based at Moorabbin Airport — Victoria’s top hub for general aviation — GowldWings benefits from world-class flight training conditions.

With five active runways, 13km+ of sealed surfaces, and a network of over 40 aviation businesses (covering flight ops, maintenance, simulators, and testing), students experience a professional training environment.

Hosting around 250,000 movements annually — most of which are training — Moorabbin remains curfew-free and operates 24/7. Add to that a dedicated pilot shop, and it's easy to see why GoldWings is the ideal place to launch your aviation career.
