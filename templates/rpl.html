Where Do You Begin?

The Recreational Pilot Licence (RPL) marks your first official step in the aviation world. It enables you to operate light aircraft solo or with passengers within 25 nautical miles of your take-off location, with options to expand your privileges through endorsements.

Whether you’re flying for leisure or progressing toward a commercial career, the RPL builds a solid and practical flying foundation.

Diploma of Aviation – Your CPL Pathway

Dreaming of a career in aviation? The Diploma of Aviation offers a direct and structured path to your Commercial Pilot Licence.

The program begins with RPL training and continues through advanced licensing in a full-time study format.
